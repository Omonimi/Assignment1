Required Ingredients For Banga Soup


Serving: 8 Persons.

1. Meat (1kg)
2. Ground Crayfish (half cup)
3. Fresh prawn [oporo](one cup)
4.Fresh Palm Fruits (Banga) (10 cups)
5. 1 Medium-sized dry fish / smoked fish
6. Red scotch bonnet pepper “Ata rodo” (6)
7. Ataiko (1 teaspoon)
8.Irugege (1 teaspoon)
9. Oburunbebe Stick (Banga stick) (1)
10. crushed dried beletientien leaves (half cup)
11.3 seasoning cubes
12.1 cup shelled Periwinkles
13.Salt to taste

Preparation


Step 1

Start by grinding the spices. Add them all together (ataiko, Irugege and a cup of crayfish), blend to powder. Blend the pepper as well. Wash the dried fish and remove center bones. I used dried catfish. This catfish is actually my favorite, it tastes so wonderful in soups. You need just one. Pour hot water over it, wash and set aside.

Step 2

Wash and precook the prawn, use half a cup of water, a seasoning cube and a pinch of salt.
Precook your meat, this is how – Wash properly and use two seasoning cubes and a pinch of salt.
Cook the meat until it becomes easy to chew, and the water is almost dried. Add salt to taste and allow another three minutes.
Set aside.

Step 3

Boil the palm fruit for 20 minutes, pound with a mortar and pestle, transfer into a bowl,
add water and extract the juice, don’t add too much water, you want it thick.
You will need about 7-10 cups of that palm fruit juice for this soup. It should be thick.
